Scrambled Eggs

Serves 4

These eggs cook very quickly, so it's important everyone is ready to eat before you start to cook them.

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon table salt
  • Pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs, milk salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl until any streaks are gone and the color is pure yellow.

  2. Melt the butter in a 10-inch nonstick skillet over high heat, swirling to coat the pan.

  3. Add the eggs and, using a heatproof rubber spatula, cook while gently pushing, lifting, and folding them from one side of the pan to the other as they form curds

  4. Continue until the eggs are nicely clumped into a single mound but remain shiny and wet, 1 1/2 to 2 minutes. Serve.
